Recipie Title

PB, Banana and Fluff Sandwich

A super yummy banana, peanut butter, and fluff snadwich - perfect for summer picnics.

Ingredients

Sandwich:

  • White Bread (2 slices)
  • 3 labespoons of peanut butter
  • 1 scliced banana
  • 2 tabespoons of Marshmallow Fluff

Directions

  1. Place bread on place
    • Spread peanut butter on bottom piece
    • Spread fluff on top piece
    • Place banana slices on peanut butter
  2. Put pieces of bread together
  3. Cut in half with knife
  4. Enjoy :) Serve with Milk

Prep Time: 5 minutes
Cook Time: 5-7 minutes
